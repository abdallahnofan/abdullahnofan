About Me

Abdallah Nofan is an ambitious ethical hacker raised in Jordan, where his passion for technology and problem-solving began. His journey led him to uncover critical vulnerabilities in systems, earning him international recognition in the bug bounty community.

After making significant contributions, he relocated to the UK to work at Meta’s London office, specifically at King’s Cross, where he continues to tackle complex cybersecurity challenges. Simultaneously, he is pursuing his sixth year in medical school, blending his expertise in technology and medicine. Abdallah's mission is to innovate and protect, ensuring safety in both digital and healthcare landscapes.
